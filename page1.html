Understanding Cell Types

All living organisms are made of cells. There are two main types of cells.

Prokaryotic Cells

Simple cells that do not have a nucleus.

Prokaryotic cells are small and simple. Their DNA floats freely in the cell. Most bacteria are prokaryotic cells.

Eukaryotic Cells

More complex cells with a nucleus.

Eukaryotic cells contain a nucleus and organelles such as mitochondria. Animals, plants, fungi, and protists are made of these cells.
